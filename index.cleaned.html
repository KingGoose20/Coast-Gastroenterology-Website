Dr Griff Walker

Dr Griff Walker is a specialist Gastroenterologist practicing on the Gold Coast, Queensland.  Dr Walker consults at the Gold Coast Private Hospital in Southport. Dr Walker is a Visiting Medical Officer at the Gold Coast Private Hospital in Southport and Pindara Private Hospital at Benowa. Dr Walker is fully accredited with the Gastroenterology Society of Australia (GESA) and is a Fellow of the Royal Australasian College of Physicians (RACP).
